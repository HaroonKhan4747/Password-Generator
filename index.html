Why a Business Name Matters

Your business name is the foundation of your brand. It’s what people remember and how they find you online. A great name builds trust, credibility, and recognition.

Naming Strategies That Work

  • Use simple and clear words.
  • Make it easy to spell and pronounce.
  • Ensure the domain name is available.
  • Reflect your niche or brand values.
